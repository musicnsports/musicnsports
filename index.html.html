Community Guidelines

1. Keep it Sports + Music: All posts must pair sports content with music. Clips, highlights, edits, or original footage welcome.

2. Respect Everyone: No hate speech, harassment, or personal attacks on athletes, artists, or users. Debate the play, not the person.

3. Own What You Post: Only share content you created or have rights to use. Credit creators when possible.

4. No NSFW / Illegal: No nudity, graphic gore, or illegal activity. This is a hype feed, not a shock site.

5. No Spam or Duplicates: One video, one post. No bots, fake engagement, or mass reposts.

We reserve the right to remove content or suspend accounts that violate these rules. Play fair and keep the vibe high.

FAQ

Is the website free to use?

YES. Absolutely FREE. No paywalls, no premium tiers. Post, like, comment, and follow without spending a dime.

What types of videos can I post?

Any sports related content with music. From the most popular sports to dance competitions and hot dog eating contests. Anything recognized as a sport. If it is competitive and has a beat, it belongs here.

Do I need to upload files?

No. Just paste a YouTube or Vimeo link. We embed it so you do not host anything.

Can I post the same video twice?

No. Our system blocks duplicate YouTube / Vimeo IDs to keep the feed fresh.
